PORTLANDIA-VIVEC — FLOOR 3 EAST PARALLAX (facing Floor 4 gate)

PARALLAX LAYERS (back → front):
Layer 0 — Sky gradient (frontier dusk: deep indigo zenith → amber-haze horizon)
Layer 1 — Mt. Tabor peak (depth 0.95) — single volcanic cone, Portland's Hood analog. Distant, misty.
Layer 2 — Canton skyline (depth 0.85) — Vivec megablocks: massive flat-top step pyramids, bridge tower pylons, temple spire at center. The "impossible city."
Layer 3 — Industrial waterfront (depth 0.7) — cranes, smokestacks, bridge spans (Burnside/Steel analogs), warehouse rooflines. Warm amber glow from below.
Layer 4 — Grand Arch facade (depth 0.5) — the Floor 4 gate. Massive ceremonial arch flanked by shorter wall, visible at screen center. Warm lantern spill.
Water band — harbor reflection strip below horizon, connects boardwalk to city

DESIGN INTENT: Player stands on Floor 3 frontier (cool, sparse) and looks east toward civilization's peak. The city is warm amber against cool indigo sky — a beacon pulling you forward. Canton blocks read as alien megastructure (Vivec) but the cranes/bridges ground it as Portland waterfront. The grand arch is the focal point — it's where Floor 4 begins.
